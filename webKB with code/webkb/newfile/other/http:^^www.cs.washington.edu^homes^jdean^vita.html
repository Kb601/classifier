profile-guided optimization for object-oriented languages [grove et al. 95], and improved automatic inlining decisions [dean & chambers 94]. 6/92 to 9/92: instructor, dept. of computer science & engineering, university of washington. 9/91 to 6/92: teaching assistant, dept. of computer science & engineering, univ. of washington. see below for description of teaching experience. 6/90 to 9/91: member of technical staff, global programme on aids, world health organization, geneva, switzerland . designed and implemented software for manipulating & analyzing statistical data [epi info 6.0].
