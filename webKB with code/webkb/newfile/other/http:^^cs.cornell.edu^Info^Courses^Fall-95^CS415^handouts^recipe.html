the frougal gourmet penne ai broccoli penne ai broccoli: pasta; sauce; pecorino cheese. add the sauce to the pasta. grate fresh pecorino cheese (not the pre-grated stuff!) on top of it. buon appetito! pasta: water; salt; penne; broccoli. bring water to boil; add salt, broccoli florets, and penne. stir well. drain when al dente (about 10 minutes, if you bought a good brand of pasta). sauce: extra-vergin olive oil; garlic; chili peppers. in extra vergin olive oil, brown 3 or 4 cloves of garlic and a couple of chili peppers. remove cloves and peppers before serving.
