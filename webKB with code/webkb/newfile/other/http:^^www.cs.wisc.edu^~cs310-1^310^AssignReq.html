cs310 assignment requirements turning in your homework when turning in homework, put your name and lab section number in the upper right hand corner of the first page. you will lose 5 points if you don't do this. put your answers to the questions together on a separate sheet of paper. if the ta grading your paper can not easily find your answers, you will lose points. homework is due at the end of the class period. no late homework is accepted. grading policy the two midterms and the final are each 20% of the final grade. the assignment score is 40% of the final grade. the assignment score is the sum of the highest 7 assignment scores out of the 8 assignments. do your own work! each student must do his or her own work. it is considered cheating to turn in all or part of someone else's work as your own. you may discuss homework problems with other students, but you are not to copy anyone else's written work or code. you must not do work for another student. penalties for cheating will be imposed.
