wisconsin cow wisconsin cow wisconsin cluster of workstations (cow) is a collection of 40 sun sparcstation-20s (soon to be) connected by a high-speed network (probably myricom myrinet). each workstation has two ross hypersparc processors, 64 mb of memory, and two 1-gb disks. those with an eye to computer history may be amused that the cow is housed in venerable pdp-11 racks. last updated by mark d. hill on fri feb 3 11:12:43 cst 1995
