richard f. riesenfeld richard f. riesenfeld professor of computer science. co-head, with elaine cohen, of the alpha_1 project. principal investigator and co-director of the national science foundation science and technology center for computer graphics and scientific visualization. current areas of interest and research: algorithms, representations, and high level design operators for complex models in computer graphics, geometric modeling, and mechanical design. integrated process planning and computer aided manufacturing. new paradigms for modeling. alpha_1 research group, university of utah
